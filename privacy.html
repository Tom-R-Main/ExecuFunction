Privacy

We collect the minimum we need for the waitlist (email + timestamp + consent). Endpoints sending or receiving PII are marked no‑store and are never cacheable by intermediaries.

What we collect

  • Waitlist emails and timestamps (if you join)
  • Ritual check-in entries (mood + short note)
  • Task entries (title + next action)
  • All stored as minimal records in Azure

Your rights

  • Request deletion at any time—we'll remove your records from storage
  • No third-party sharing or advertising
  • You'll only hear from us about WinonaOS milestones and early access
  • All personal data endpoints use no-store headers for privacy
